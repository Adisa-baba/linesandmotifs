About Us

Lines & Motifs™ Interior Design and Consultancy Services Ltd. is a full-service architecture, interior building solutions, and furniture manufacturing company, registered under the laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria. Founded in November 2016 and located at 9b Adekoya Street, off Ajose, Mende-Maryland, Lagos, we have built a strong reputation for transforming spaces through design excellence, skilled craftsmanship, and client-centered service.
